Fasolakia

Description

Authentic Greek green beans, braised with potatoes in a tomato and olive oil sauce. Perfect garnished with basil and paired with feta and crusty bread.

Ingredients

  • 500g green beans, trimmed
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium potato, chopped into large pieces
  • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
  • 100ml olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Chopped parsley, to garnish

Method

  1. Heat olive at medium to low heat. Add onion and sauté until soft.
  2. Add potatoes and sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add beans and stir until covered in oil.
  5. Add tomatoes, parsley, sugar and salt and pepper and stir together.
  6. Simmer with lid on for around 40 minutes. It's ready once the beans are soft.
  7. Enjoy garnished with parsley.
