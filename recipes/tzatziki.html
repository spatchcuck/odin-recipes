Tzatziki

Description

Classic, creamy, Greek cucumber yoghurt dip, delicious with bread or spooned onto roast lamb.

Ingredients

  • 500ml Greek yoghurt
  • 1/2 English cucumber, peeled in stripes
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • Handful of chopped dill or mint (or both)
  • 1/4 tsp ground white pepper
  • 1 tsp salt

Method

  1. Grate or food process the cucumber. Toss with 1/2 tsp salt, spoon into cheese cloth and squeeze tightly until dry.
  2. In a large bowl, combine garlic with remaining salt, and olive oil and mix to combine.
  3. Add grated cucumber, yoghurt, pepper and herbs and stir to combine. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Before serving, drizzle the top of the tzatziki with a little more olive oil.
